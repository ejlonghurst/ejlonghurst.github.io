Trilogy
Introduction

creative inspiration and journey

Nature is all around us, beautifying and strengthening our world from oceans and rainforests to park trees and window boxes; she is our home and provider, and speaking for the writers, gardeners and dreamers for the future — our love. But still the stark fact stands that only 23% of the world’s landmass, and only about 13% of the ocean can still be classified as wilderness. Humans have invaded, changed and destroyed many valuable and irreplaceable parts of Nature. But these books are a testament to her, and most importantly an expression of my strong faith in the generations of today to help our species care better for our mother. The voices of the young people of today are powerful, and though their powers might not be Magical, the real magic that we can work with is each other.
